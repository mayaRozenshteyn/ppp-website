WHAT?

Plant-Powered Plates aims to inspire kids to combat climate change through sustainable food choices.

HOW?

Using kid-friendly and engaging videos, handouts, and activities, we hope to motivate children to create positive environmental change through their everyday eating habits.

HOW EXACTLY?

Specifically, our initiative includes the Plant-Powered Plates Challenge, a school-wide competition encouraging kids to create their own plant-based entrees, some of which can be incorporated into school lunch menus. Through this challenge and our other climate-related activities and lessons, we hope to empower children to spread environmental awareness and activism in their schools, families, and communities. We also hope that our program will encourage more nutritious and sustainable options in school lunch menus, ultimately fostering a generation of healthier and more environmentally-conscious citizens.
